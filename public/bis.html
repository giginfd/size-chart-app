Save Sync Bookmark Open Omnisend Report
1
Save the Sync Bookmark
Right-click the Save Sync Bookmark button above and choose Bookmark Link. Save it in your bookmarks bar as SYNC BIS.
2
Open the Omnisend report
Click Open Omnisend Report above and wait until the page finishes loading completely.
3
Run the SYNC BIS bookmark
While on the Omnisend page, click your saved SYNC BIS bookmark. The sync may take about 1–2 minutes. Wait for the confirmation popup.
4
Return to this dashboard
After the popup confirms the sync, return here. The BIS data will now be updated.
